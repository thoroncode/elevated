This is a faithful port of "Elevated" by rgba & TBC, an award-winning 4KB demoscene intro from Breakpoint 2009. The app is a self-playing procedural terrain flythrough with synthesized music — there is no user interaction required. The demo runs approximately 3:37 and loops automatically. Press Select or Play/Pause on the Siri Remote to pause/resume. Swipe on the touchpad to scrub.
